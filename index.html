Welcome to Minesweeper!

The objective of the game is to uncover all the tiles that do not contain mines

Uncover a mine and you lose the game

Choose the difficulty level

Click on the tiles to reveal them

The number on the tile shows how many adjacent tiles are mines

Click and hold to place a flag on tiles that you think are mines

Do the same to remove the flag

Try to uncover the board without revealing a mine

Good Luck!
