How To Play

Click on the tiles to reveal them

The number on the tile shows how many adjacent tiles are mines

Click and hold to place a flag on tile that you think are mines

Do the same to remove the flag

Try to uncover the board without revealing a mine

Good Luck!

Keyboard Controls

W A S D -> Move cursor

Q -> Reveal tile

E -> Place/remove flag
